Christmas Lights

Hypothesis

How do my emotions shift from morning to night over time? Is my social life conected to my mood pattern?

Elements

Just like a real string of lights, each bulb represents a day. The wire is the timeline, and the colors reflect how I felt. It's a playful and emotional way to show the ups and downs of the month.

Pattern

At a first glance it doesn't look like there's a strong pattern. But when looking at the days of the week, something interesting appears: Tuesdays and Wednesdays tend to have the lowest vibes compared to the rest. It's a detail I didn't expect to notice until I look closely.

Why I Chose This?

I wanted to create something fun and festive, inspired by decorative string lights. It felt fitting for the mood-tracking project and honestly made me happy when the visualization finally started to look like an actual light installation.
