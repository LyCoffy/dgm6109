Gluten Free Pancakes

Soft, warm, and naturally comforting. These pancakes stay gentle on your stomach while giving you a cozy morning start. Perfect for anyone wanting something fluffy, simple, and delicious.

Suggestions to make your pancakes even better

Quick tips to improve texture:

Mix your dry ingredients thoroughly to avoid dense spots in the batter. Let the mixture rest for 5 minutes before cooking—this helps the flour hydrate and makes the pancakes softer and more tender.

How to customize the flavor:

Add cinnamon, banana slices, or blueberries directly into the batter for extra sweetness. A small dash of vanilla also makes the pancakes smell amazing and feel more comforting.

Instructions

Mix your dry ingredients in a medium bowl—gluten free flour, baking powder, and sugar. Stir gently to combine and create a smooth base before adding anything wet. This prevents clumps and helps the pancakes rise evenly.

Add the milk, egg, and melted butter. Mix until just combined. Avoid overmixing—small lumps mean fluffier pancakes. Let the batter sit for a few minutes so it thickens naturally.

Heat a lightly buttered pan over medium heat. Pour a small ladle of batter and watch for tiny bubbles forming on top. This is your cue to flip. The second side cooks faster and browns beautifully.

Serve warm and top with fruits, honey, sugar glass, or anything comforting. These pancakes taste best fresh, soft, and airy—perfect for a gentle morning.
