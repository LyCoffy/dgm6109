Why consider eating vegan?

What does Vegan mean?

Being vegan means choosing foods that come entirely from plants. This includes fruits, vegetables, nuts, grains, seeds, and legumes. Vegan meals avoid meat, eggs, dairy, and other animal-derived ingredients. It is a simple and flexible way to explore lighter meals that tend to be easier on digestion.

Many people enjoy vegan meals because they feel cleaner, fresher, and naturally energizing.

Why you might benefit from it:

Plant-based foods are naturally rich in fiber and gentle nutrients that support your metabolism, digestion, and overall comfort. For some people, removing dairy or heavy proteins can significantly reduce bloating and fatigue.

Even trying vegan meals a few times a week can help you discover flavors that feel good for your body while still being satisfying and comforting.
