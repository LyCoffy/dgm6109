Why choose Low FODMAP foods?

What does Low FODMAP mean?

A Low FODMAP diet focuses on reducing certain types of carbohydrates that can be difficult for some people to digest. These carbs are found in foods like onions, garlic, some fruits, dairy, and wheat. By lowering these ingredients, many people experience less bloating, gas, and discomfort.

It is commonly recommended for those dealing with digestive sensitivities.

Why it might help you:

Many people feel immediate relief when eating Low FODMAP meals. The focus on simple ingredients helps reduce digestive stress and increases overall comfort. Even if you don’t follow it strictly, choosing Low FODMAP meals a few times a week can help you understand what your body responds well to.

It is a gentle, approachable way to learn more about your digestion and support your wellbeing.
