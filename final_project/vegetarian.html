Why consider eating vegetarian?

What does Vegetarian mean?

A vegetarian diet focuses on plant-based foods like vegetables, grains, fruits, nuts, beans, and legumes. Unlike veganism, vegetarians can also enjoy dairy and eggs, making it a flexible and approachable lifestyle.

It’s a way of eating that emphasizes lighter meals that tend to be easier for many people to digest.

Why it might help you:

Vegetarian meals often feel more energizing and less heavy than meals centered around red or processed meats. Many people experience less bloating, smoother digestion, and more stable energy throughout the day.

Even adding a few vegetarian dishes per week can help you feel lighter, balanced, and more comfortable.
